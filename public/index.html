Cubul is an educational toy for children from Kindergarten to Middle School. It playfully teaches letters and word formation, enhancing mental, creative, and motor skills. Vowels and consonants are in different colors, helping children subconsciously understand their differences. Cubul is high-quality, easy to store, and requires no instructions, making it perfect for school, home, or travel.

Our Ambition

Durable and reusable

Bricks, tiles and box are made out of ABS material, which is lightweight and makes it last longer.

Compatible with other brick systems

Combine the bricks with other brands to boost your fun.

Easy to understand

It is intuitive for kids to open the box and start building words.

For School

The box is designed to fit easy into a schoolbag to transport to and back from school every day.

At Home

Use the box at home during reading to learn new words and memorise them.

For Travel

Being light-weight and small, the box and the letters can be easily taken with you while exploring the word.
